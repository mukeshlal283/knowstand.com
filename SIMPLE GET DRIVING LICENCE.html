Driving licence

We already know about driving licence, but some people do not know how to make driving licence, but don't worry today i will give you all information about how to apply for driving licence. Diving licence is a proof which allow you to drive vehicle but not all class of vehicle only those type of vehicle which is written on your driving licence.

You will understand please read all step:

STEP 1: Firstlly open website sarthi pariwahan or click here and select state.

STEP 2: Click apply online (remember one thing, firstlly apply learner licence after you get learner licence then you apply for driving licence) then click new learner licence.

STEP 3: new page open in your screen click continue then new page open select "applicant does not hold driving/learner licence" and click submit.

STEP 4: enter mobile number and OTP then click "authentication". New page will be open firstlly select "state" then enter your pincode your column of "RTO Office" is automatically selected according to your pincode.

STEP 5: Enter all personal detail like NAME, in RELATION (choose your related person given in "select" option), gender, qualification etc.

STEP 6: Write your "ADDRESS" correct because Authority send your driving licence through SPEED POST also write correct mobile number.

STEP 7: Now select "class of vehicle" , Some people get confused in this option before select option please read the table are given below:

CLASS OF VEHICLE INFORMATION ABOUT CLASS OF VEHICLE
MCWOG motor cycle without gear like "scooter"
MCWG motor cycle with gear like "bike"
LMV light motor vehicle include "car or vans"
transport vehicle used for provide services like good carriage
non transport vehicle for personal use like car, bike etc.

If you want to drive car and bike both choose MCWG and LMV

click "submit".

STEP 8: As you can see in this picture you will get "APPLICATION NUMBER" and print out this receipt and click "next".

STEP 9: now next step is "PAYMENT". See the picture given below:

it is necessary to print all these three document (acknowledgement, form 1, form 1-a), go downward and click "payment".

step 10: After "payment" the next step is "UPLOAD DOCUMENT" age proof and address proof

STEP 11: Upload document of "AGE PROOF" and "ADDRESS PROOF". Then click "next".

STEP 12: Now upload "PHOTO" and "SIGNATURE".

STEP 13:"SLOOT BOOKING" at the last you need to book the sloot for test, select DATE AND TIMING.

IMPORTANT NOTE
1. Applicant must carry A PAYMENT RECEIPT, ATLEAST 4 PHOTOGRAPHS, REAL COPY AND PHOTOCOPY OF ADDRESS PROOF AND AGE PROOF, SLOT BOOKING RECEIPT AND ALL THESE THREE DOCUMENTS
2. You must to go RTO office 30 minute before the actual time for DOCUMENT VERIFICATION
3. You have to give a test in computer
4. You must to give atleast 6 answer correct out of 10 questions to pass and get learner licence.
5. You will get learner licence atleast 5 minute later after pass in test.
